About Us

Eventify is a modern platform designed to connect people with events that matter. Whether you're a student, professional, or enthusiast, we help you discover workshops, seminars, cultural fests, and online meetups with ease.

Our mission is to simplify the event registration experience while supporting event organizers with smart tools to promote, manage, and grow their audience. From college festivals to global conferences, we aim to be your go-to destination for everything events.

Founded by a group of students and developers passionate about community-building, Eventify believes in the power of events to educate, inspire, and connect people across all walks of life.
